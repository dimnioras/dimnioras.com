Transit Planner, Spatial Analyst, Software Developer

Welcome to my website!

I am a Transportation Planner and Spatial Analyst focusing in Transit and Sustainable Development. I am holding a B.Sc. (5-year diploma) in Rural and Surveying Engineering at National Technical University of Athens and am pursuing a M.Eng. in Transportation Engineering at Illinois Institute of Technology. I am also currently working as a Transportation Planning Intern in Chicago Metropolitan Agency for Planning (CMAP).

My interests include Transit Planning, Management and Operations, Railway, Aviation and Urban Planning and Development, as well as GIS, Technology, Software Development and Music. My goal is to combine my skills and interests in a way that promotes sustainable development, focusing in transit and mass transportation in general.

Check my website for information about my projects and share your thoughts .

Athens Public Transport History Archive (External Link)

This directory will host one of the biggest projects that I am trying to create. It will feature a dynamic map of the Athens area, in which someone can take a look at the history of certain bus, trolleybus and tram lines at different times, or view a snapshot of the network at a specific day in the past (as long as data exists). I will provide more information about the structure in the future; you are welcome to help me accomplish this big project!

For now, you can find a collection of maps and brochures from 1979 to date.

Paper Bus Models (External Link)

This directory holds one of the first hobby projects that I started and decided to share. You can find designs of the public transport buses that are being used in Athens, as well as the white templates to create your own. The bus models can be printed and made at home (photo paper recommended).
